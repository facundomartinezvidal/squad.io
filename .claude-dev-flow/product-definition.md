# Squad.io — Product Definition

## Core Transaction (The Verb)

*Connect* — The app connects players who have an incomplete group with available players looking to join a match. The player who needs to complete their group posts a request, and available players can apply to fill that spot.

## Acquisition Plan

To prevent the app from being empty at launch, subsidize *solo players (free agents)*:
- Offer in-app rewards (reputation points, badges) to the first players who register as "available"
- Discord integration to import existing gaming communities
- Partnerships with small/mid-tier streamers who frequently need to fill lobbies
- Groups posting requests arrive organically because they have the immediate need

## Matchmaking Criteria

The most important data point for matching two people is *competitive rank/level*:
- **Rank** (e.g., Gold, Platinum, Diamond in LoL/Valorant) — nobody wants to play with someone 3 ranks below or above
- **Role/Position** — if the team needs a support, match with players who play support
- **Language** — communication is key in competitive games
- **Server/Region** — to ensure good ping and latency

## User Tools

- **Quick search system**: post that you need a player with filters (game, rank, role, language)
- **Player profile**: stats imported from the game's API (rank, winrate, main roles)
- **Real-time chat**: to coordinate before entering the match
- **Reputation system**: rate the player after the match (punctuality, attitude, skill)
- **Shared match history**: see who you played with and repeat if it was a good experience
- **Favorites list**: save reliable players to invite in the future

## Community Guidelines (Governance)

### 3 Golden Rules (immediate ban if broken):

1. **No toxicity or harassment**: Insults, discrimination, or any form of harassment towards other players results in a permanent ban. The platform is meant to be a good time.
2. **No match ghosting**: If you accept to join a group and don't show up (without notice), it's considered a serious offense. Reliability is the core of the app.
3. **No smurfing or rank falsification**: Lying about your real rank or using alt accounts to appear as a different level breaks matchmaking and ruins the experience for everyone.
